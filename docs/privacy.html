YOUNGER

Privacy Policy

Effective June 9, 2026

What Younger accesses

Younger may access health and fitness information you authorize through Apple Health and WHOOP, including activity, sleep, recovery, workout, heart-rate, and body-measurement data.

How data is used

Your data is used only to calculate and display daily wellness progress, trends, and suggested actions inside Younger. Younger does not use health data for advertising and does not sell health data.

Storage and security

Apple Health data is read on your device. WHOOP OAuth credentials are stored in a restricted Firebase database and are accessed only by the Younger backend. WHOOP client secrets are stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager. The mobile app receives an opaque Younger session token rather than your WHOOP refresh token.

Sharing

Younger shares information only with service providers needed to operate the integration, such as Google Firebase and WHOOP, or when legally required. Younger does not provide health data to data brokers.

Your choices

You can revoke Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings. You can disconnect WHOOP inside Younger, which deletes the Younger session and stored WHOOP connection. You may also revoke Younger from your WHOOP account.

Retention

WHOOP credentials are retained while your account remains connected. Temporary OAuth state and connection tickets expire within minutes and can be used only once.

Wellness disclaimer

Younger is a general wellness tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or a clinical biological-age assessment.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Younger develops. The effective date above will change when material revisions are published.
